# Code of Conduct

**Weaver Forge — Daily Commit Lab** is a builder community for technical growth, accountability, and evidence-backed progress.

### Our Standard
**Respect people. Challenge claims. Build with evidence.**

### Expected Behavior
- Treat others with respect
- Ask questions in good faith
- Give technical feedback clearly and constructively
- Challenge claims without attacking people
- Share evidence when making progress claims
- Credit others for their work
- Document failures honestly
- Keep discussions relevant to learning, building, and reviewing

### Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, insults, threats, or personal attacks
- Discrimination based on identity, background, nationality, gender, religion, disability, or experience level
- Spam, scams, low-effort self-promotion, or course-selling without permission
- Plagiarism or presenting others’ work as your own
- Fake progress claims
- Sharing private code, private data, credentials, or personal information
- Pressuring members to reveal personal details
- Using the community to misrepresent credentials or project validation

### Technical Review Standard
We review work, not worth.  
A failed experiment is acceptable when documented.  
A strong claim requires strong evidence.  
A contributor cannot witness their own artifact.

### Enforcement
Maintainers may issue warnings, remove posts, limit participation, or remove members. Serious issues will be escalated immediately.

### Reporting
Report issues to a maintainer with:
- What happened
- Where it happened
- Screenshots or links
- Relevant context

Reports will be handled with discretion.

---

**Build. Test. Commit. Receipt. Repeat.**  
**No commit. No claim. No receipt. No authority.**
